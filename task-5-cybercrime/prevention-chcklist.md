 Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

General Safety Tips
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible  
- Do not click on suspicious links or unknown emails  
- Always verify website URLs before entering personal details  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities  

 UPI / Financial Safety
- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone  
- Do not accept unknown payment requests or “collect requests”  
- Verify QR codes before scanning for payment  
- Always double-check receiver details before sending money  

 Reporting Cybercrime (India)
- Report incidents at: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Call Cyber Helpline: 1930 immediately for financial fraud  

 Reminder
Staying alert and cautious online is the best way to prevent cybercrime.
